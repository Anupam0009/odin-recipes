Chicken Curry

Description

It's an Indian dish. It's also spicy and needed rice or bread to eat with.

Various aromatic powder are used to season the dish. It's also relatively easy to make.

Ingrediends

  • chiken
  • potato
  • curry powder
  • onion
  • Tomatoes

Step

  1. Satue the sliced onion with oil in a pan.
  2. Add the curry powder and cook untill it separates from oil.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook with some water.
  4. Then pour in the chicken and cook it 30 minutes.
