Lasagna

Description

You need cooked meat, chease, and cook noodles. Then lear them and fry them in oven

Ingrediends

  • meat
  • Cheese
  • Noddles
  • Tomatoes
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Mix tomato purey, onions, ginger-galik pest with the meat.
  3. Cook the Lasagna noodles with salty boiled water.
  4. Mix egg with cheese.
  5. Layer them one after another with chesse on the top
  6. Repeat the Layer
  7. Put them into the oven for 29 minutes.
